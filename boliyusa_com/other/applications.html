CAUTION

  • Be sure the total load is within generator rated output otherwise generator damage may occur.
  • Do not use AC and DC power at the same time or the generator may be damaged.

    • Note:

    Some precision equipment is voltage sensitive and may require a more uniform voltage supply that portable generators. Examples include some medical equipment, personal computers, and some inverters that sense peak and RMS voltage values. Consult the precision-equipment vendor before relying on any portable generator to provide power to such equipment.
